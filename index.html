Who We Are

Johar Jaya is a natural stone company with a strong foundation built on experience and craftsmanship. We began our natural stone production in 2003, driven by a passion for Indonesia’s rich stone resources and a commitment to quality in every detail. Over the years, our hands-on approach and deep understanding of materials have allowed us to consistently deliver reliable and well-crafted products. In 2017, we took an important step forward by expanding into the export market, bringing our natural stone to international clients. Today, Johar Jaya continues to grow with a personal commitment to quality, long-term partnerships, and delivering natural stone solutions that our customers can trust.
