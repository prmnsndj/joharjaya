Product's Frequently Asked Questions
What is the difference between Beige Palimanan, Yellow Palimanan, and Yellow Sandstone? +

While the three stones may appear similar, they differ in color tones and geological composition. Beige Palimanan has a lighter, less stripes and creamier hue compared to the warmer tones of Yellow Palimanan and Yellow Sandstone.
