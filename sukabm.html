What Is Bali Sukabumi Green ?

Sukabumi Stone also known as "Pedra Hijau" is an exquisite natural quartzite found exclusively in Indonesia. Renowned for its distinct emerald hues and theraputic properties, it has become the gold standard for luxury swimming pools, high-end spas, and modern architectural landscapes globally.

Key Features & Benefits

Natural Water Purification : This stone contains Zeolite, a natural mineral that helps purify water by absorbing heavy metals and reducing ammonia levels. This leads to a cleaner, and healthier swimming

Distinctive Aesthetics : When dry, the stone displays a soft and earthy green. Once submerged, it transforms into a vibrant, deep emerald that creates an exotic, tropical lagoon effect.

Thermal Stability : Unlike many synthetic materials, Sukabumi stone remains cool to the touch even under direct sunlight, providing a comfortable surface for pool decks and walkaways.

Safety & Durability : Naturally slip-resistant (especially in "honed" or "split face" finishes) and highly resistant to corrosion and thermal shock, ensuring a long-lasting finish in high-moisture areas.
